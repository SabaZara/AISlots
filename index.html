Win on the lines

Match three or more identical symbols on adjacent reels, beginning on the leftmost reel. Your total bet is divided equally across 20 fixed lines. More than one line may win on the same spin.

Moonwell Bloom bonus

Bloom symbols do not pay on lines. Every Bloom anywhere on the grid fills one permanent meter position. At 12, the Moonwell reveals three independently weighted constellation prizes, then the meter rolls over.

Prizes are multiplied by the total bet and added together.

Algorithm
