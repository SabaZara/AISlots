Win on the lines

Match three or more identical symbols on adjacent reels, beginning on the leftmost reel. Your total bet is divided equally across 25 fixed lines. More than one line may win on the same spin.

Scatter Flight bonus

Scatter symbols do not pay on lines. Every Scatter anywhere on the grid fills one permanent meter position. At 18, the vault launches three independently weighted multiplier flights, then the meter rolls over.

Prizes are multiplied by the total bet and added together.

Algorithm

Random bytes come from SHA-256(serverSeed:clientSeed:nonce:counter). Four-byte values are converted with rejection sampling, avoiding modulo bias. The open source model maps those values to the published symbol and bonus weights.
